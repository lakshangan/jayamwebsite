About Sahaya selvam


Introduction to the Founder of Jayam Fashion Institution: Sahaya Selvam Sahaya Selvam, the visionary founder of Jayam Fashion Institution, brings over 22 years of unparalleled expertise in the art of tailoring. With a profound dedication to the craft, Sahaya has honed her skills and knowledge through a distinguished educational background. She holds a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration (B.B.A.) from Anna Malai University and has completed the prestigious CYP (Common Young Program). Her academic journey also includes a CMD (Commercial Master Designer) certification from Chennai's renowned St. Joseph's College.

Sahaya's commitment to excellence is further demonstrated through her ability to provide government registration certifications and her affiliation with the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC). Her versatile talents extend beyond tailoring, as she is also a certified beautician specializing in therapeutic massage.

At Jayam Fashion Institution, Sahaya Selvam combines her extensive industry experience and comprehensive educational achievements to inspire and train the next generation of fashion professionals. Her leadership and expertise ensure that students receive top-tier education and practical skills, preparing them for successful careers in the dynamic world of fashion and beauty.
